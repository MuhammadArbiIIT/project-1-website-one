Fact 1:Largest city in Pakistan is Karachi.

Karachi is one of the largest cities in the world coming number 12 on the list of largest cities in the world. This city has a population of almost 15 million people. Another fact about this city is that I was born here and lived their till I was 6 years old. Karachi is a amazing city and hosts a lot of attractions and activities for a person to do.

Fact 2: There are 75 spoken languages in Pakistan!

Having a vast population of 200 million plus people makes a country have different people speaking different languages. This is the case in Pakistan as it was once part of India and Bangladesh but it was put into its own seperate country in 1947. This resulted in many people migrating to Pakistan and speaking the languages they spoke from India or Bangladesh. This resulted in many different people knowing multiple languages as it was necessary in order for a person to communicate with another person.

Fact 3: The most popular sport in Pakistan is Cricket.

The most known and played sport in Pakistan is called Cricket which is a form of baseball but there are different bats and different rules in some aspects. The sport is very fun to play and watch and I used to play it as a kid when I used to live in Pakistan. The sport gets national attention from all over the world and everyone has to watch the match of their favorite teams as it is very competitive. The national team also won the Cricket World Cup in 1992 and were World Twenty20 champions in 2009.

Fact 4: The official currency in Pakistan is the rupee.

The rupee is a national currency that is used by many countries such as India, Bangladesh, Maldives, Sri Lanka and many more. These countries use the currency as one point in time India was a major country that was connected to other South Asian countries which made them adopt the currency. Over time these countries change their national currency to rupees allowing it to be used in many parts of South Asia.
