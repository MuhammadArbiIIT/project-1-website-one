Virtual Machines were a byproduct of computers and what they were capable of achieving. To see and use this website, a person would need a computer and a internet connection. This lead to many different companies making it easy for the usser to operate their computers by making software. The software that they made was used to operate a computer with ease instead of typing in a terminal to do every command.
